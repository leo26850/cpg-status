10 · Definitions

How these metrics are defined

MQL

Lead has reached Attio Leads stage Booked Call. CPG-specific override of the SOP-generic Fit+Intent ≥ 60.

SQL

Deal has reached Proposal Sent, Negotiating, or Closed Won. CPG-specific override of the SOP-generic BANT-lite.

CPL

Fully-loaded monthly spend ÷ deduped, junk-excluded leads. First-touch attribution.

CPA

Cohort-based: N−3 month spend ÷ Closed Won deals from the cohort by month N+90.
